Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released in 2014 by Google. It appears when users lose internet connection in Chrome.

Players control a dinosaur that jumps over obstacles and avoids enemies. It's simple, fun, and addictive.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game was released in 2019 by House House. It is a puzzle-stealth game where players control a mischievous goose causing chaos in a quiet village

Players especially enjoyed the freedom to annoy villagers in creative ways. It's unique, fun, and addictive.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird was released in 2013 by Dong Nguyen. It is a mobile game where players tap the screen to keep a bird flying between pipes.

The game became extremely popular for its difficulty and simplicity before it was eventually removed.
